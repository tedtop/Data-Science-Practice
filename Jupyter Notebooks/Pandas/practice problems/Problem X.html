Problem 10¶

Part 1: Greenhouse gas emissions¶

The file greenhouse_emissions.csv provides a comprehensive accounting of total greenhouse gas emissions for all man-made sources in the United States. The gases covered include carbon dioxide, methane, nitrous oxide, hydrofluorocarbons, perfluorocarbons, sulfur hexafluoride, and nitrogen trifluoride. The measurement units are in million metric tons of carbon dioxide equivalents (GT CO2 equivalent).
